# Draft a realistic 1-page case study showing how a company improved AI search visibility with Topify.ai

**How PeopleFlow Quadrupled AI Search Visibility and Turned ChatGPT Referrals into Qualified Leads**

---

**The Problem**

By early 2024, PeopleFlow — a mid-market HR automation platform serving 200–5,000 employee companies — had a serious visibility gap. While their SEO performed well on Google, a growing number of procurement conversations were starting somewhere unexpected: inside ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude. When buyers asked "best HR platforms for mid-sized teams," PeopleFlow wasn't mentioned. Competitors like Workday, BambooHR, and just-right-fit alternatives were getting cited consistently. PeopleFlow was functionally invisible in the conversations driving B2B buying decisions.

"We'd hear from prospects who'd discovered us through ChatGPT, but we had zero data on it," said Sarah Chen, Head of Growth at PeopleFlow. "Our Google Analytics showed nothing. It was a blind spot eating into pipeline."

---

**What They Tried**

The team initially tried manual monitoring — asking Sales to flag any mention of AI referrals during discovery calls. This captured maybe 10% of actual mentions and scaled poorly. They also leaned on their existing SEO stack, running Ahrefs and Moz reports to monitor traditional rankings. Helpful for Google, but useless for answering the question that mattered: *Who is ChatGPT recommending, and why?*

"We spent weeks manually prompting ChatGPT with our own keyword list and screenshotting results," Chen recalled. "It was tedious, inconsistent, and told us nothing about how to actually improve."

---

**The Solution**

PeopleFlow onboarded Topify.ai in March 2024. Within the first two weeks, they had a live dashboard tracking AI citation performance across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini — segmented by target keywords and buying-intent queries.

Topify.ai's citation analysis revealed a critical insight: PeopleFlow was being mentioned in AI responses, but only in lower-authority query variations. They were absent from high-conversion prompts like "HRIS for 500-person companies" and "HR platform with onboarding automation." Topify's AEO recommendation engine flagged specific content gaps — particularly a missing comparison page addressing the exact Workday vs. BambooHR vs. PeopleFlow decision framework that AI models were summarizing for users.

The team restructured three existing landing pages, added a structured comparison section, and integrated schema markup that Topify flagged as underutilized. They also began tracking AI citation velocity weekly, measuring not just *whether* they appeared but in what position within AI responses and alongside which competitor mentions.

---

**The Results**

By July 2024 — four months post-implementation — PeopleFlow's AI visibility metrics told a different story:

- **340% increase** in citations across tracked AI platforms for priority keywords
- **47% of inbound leads** now cite AI tools as a discovery source, up from 8%
- **2.8x improvement** in ChatGPT citation ranking for high-intent HR platform queries
- **$420,000 in attributed pipeline** from AI-referred opportunities in Q2 alone

"We went from being completely invisible in AI search to consistently appearing alongside the three biggest players in our space," Chen said. "Topify didn't just show us the problem — it gave us the specific playbook to fix it."

*Topify.ai is an AI search optimization and GEO intelligence platform that tracks, analyzes, and improves brand visibility across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, and other leading AI assistants.*
