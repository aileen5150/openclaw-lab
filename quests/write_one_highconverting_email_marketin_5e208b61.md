# Write One High-Converting Email Marketing Template

**Target Audience:** Dormant customers who haven't purchased in 60-180 days, showing signs of disengagement but still on your list.

**Intended Use Case:** Win-back campaign to re-activate lapsed customers, rekindle brand affinity, and drive repeat purchases through a compelling incentive + personalization.

---

**Subject Line:**
"We miss you, [First Name] — here's something special just for you"

*(Personalization drives 26% higher open rates; scarcity language creates urgency without feeling manipulative)*

---

**Email Body:**

Hi [First Name],

It's been a while since we've seen you.

The last time you visited, you [browsed/purchased] [specific product category or item] — we remember, because it matters to us that you found value here.

Since then, we've been busy. We've [launched X new products / introduced a new collection / improved our shipping], and we'd love to show you what's new.

But first — we want to thank you for being part of our community. Here's your exclusive reward:

**25% off your next order with code WELCOMEBACK25**
*(Valid for the next 7 days — no minimum required)*

No catch. No fine print. Just our way of saying we hope you'll give us another chance.

**[Browse what's new →]**

If [specific product from browsing history] isn't quite what you need anymore, our team is here to help you find something better. Reply to this email — we actually read and respond.

We know you have options. That's why we're committed to earning your trust, one order at a time.

Come back and see us.

Warmly,
[Your Name]
[Title]
[Company Name]

P.S. Not interested? No hard feelings — you can [unsubscribe here], but we'd hate to see you go. If you need a break, we'll keep your preference saved for when you're ready.

---

**Call-to-Action Button:**
"Claim 25% Off — Shop Now"

*(Button color:Contrasting brand accent color; placement: Above the fold, centered; copies urgency with the 7-day expiration)*

---

**Why This Template Converts:**

- **Behavior-triggered personalization** — References last purchase/browse data to feel relevant, not generic
- **Low-friction incentive** — 25% off with no minimum removes purchase barriers
- **Time-bound urgency** — The 7-day window creates action without heavy-handed manipulation
- **Human tone** — Avoids corporate jargon; feels like a real brand that values the relationship
- **Two-way exit** — P.S. acknowledges non-buyers gracefully, reducing unsubscribes and improving sender reputation

This template works especially well for e-commerce brands in retail, beauty, home goods, or lifestyle categories where repeat purchase cycles are 2-4 months. Test the subject line with A/B testing — variant B: "We saved your cart [First Name]" can perform strongly for browse abandonment segments.
