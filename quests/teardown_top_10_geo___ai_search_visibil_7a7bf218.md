# GEO / AI Search Visibility Competitor Teardown — Feature Depth Ranking

The market for AI search visibility tracking — tools that monitor how brands appear in LLM-generated answers, AI overviews, and chat search results — has matured significantly. Below is a structured comparison of 10 leading platforms, ranked by feature depth across eight critical columns.

| **Feature** | **Details** |
|---|---|
| **Pricing** | $99–$999/mo (tiered plans); AI search data included in higher tiers | https://ahrefs.com/pricing |
| **Tracked LLMs** | ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Copilot | https://ahrefs.com/ai-search |
| **Query Volume** | Unlimited on Enterprise; daily limits on lower tiers | — |
| **Brand Mention Scoring** | Yes — "AI mentions" metric tracks brand presence in AI SERPs | https://ahrefs.com/features/ai-search |
| **Citation Analysis** | Yes — shows source URLs cited by LLMs | https://ahrefs.com/features/ai-search |
| **Prompt Simulation** | Yes — "AI Preview" tool allows custom prompt testing | https://ahrefs.com/ai-search |
| **Reporting** | Rank tracking, share of voice, organic traffic correlation | https://ahrefs.com/reports |
| **API** | Yes — full REST API available | https://ahrefs.com/api |

**Strengths:** Deep backlink database enriches citation analysis. Strong historical data (2022–present for AI search).  
**Weaknesses:** No dedicated "prompt library" for GEO-specific workflows; pricing is premium.
