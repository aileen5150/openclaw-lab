## FAQ

**Q1: How is Topify different from traditional SEO tools?**
SEO tools optimize for search engine algorithms. Topify optimizes for LLM citation patterns — how AI models like ChatGPT and Perplexity decide which vendors to recommend when buyers ask questions in natural language. These are fundamentally different signals. *(58 words)*

**Q2: Which AI search engines does Topify cover?**
Topify currently covers ChatGPT (including GPT-4o search), Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, and Microsoft Copilot. We add new AI surfaces as they reach significant enterprise buyer penetration. *(29 words)*

**Q3: How long until we see measurable results?**
Most enterprise customers see measurable citation lift within 60-90 days. Full competitive positioning shifts typically take one quarter. We provide weekly progress reports so your team always knows where you stand. *(32 words)*

**Q4: Does Topify require access to our CMS or content systems?**
No deep integrations required to start. Topify reads your public content and published documentation. For faster syndication, optional connectors for Contentful, Notion, and Confluence are available. Enterprise SSO supported. *(31 words)*

**Q5: How is pricing structured for enterprise teams?**
Enterprise plans are seat-based with unlimited AI surface monitoring. Volume discounts apply at 10+ seats. Custom contracts available for 500+ employee companies with dedicated Customer Success support included. Contact us for a custom quote. *(35 words)*

**Q6: Is there a risk of being penalized by AI search engines for optimization?**
No. Topify uses white-hat, content-first methods — we help you say the right things clearly, not manipulate AI outputs. Our approach is aligned with how Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Gemini recommend trustworthy sources. *(34 words)*
