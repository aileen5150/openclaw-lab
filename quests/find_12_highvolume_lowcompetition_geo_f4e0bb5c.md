## Ranked Topic Opportunities

### 1. AI Writing Tools vs Human Writers: Complete Comparison Guide

**Target Prompt:** "Can AI writing tools replace human content writers or should I hire both?"

**SERP Snapshot:** Featured snippets dominated by tools like Jasper, Copy.ai, and Writer.com. Most results are product-specific comparison pages (e.g., "Jasper vs Copy.ai"). AI answer engines currently pull fragmented data from scattered blog posts—no definitive guide exists.

**Winning Page:** copy.ai/blog/ai-writer-vs-human-writer — 2,400 words, targets commercial intent, ranks for long-tail comparisons
